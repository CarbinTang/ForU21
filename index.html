    I Wish You Enough

    I wish you enough sun to keep your attitude bright.
    I wish you enough rain to appreciate the sun more.
    I wish you enough happiness to keep your spirits up.
    I wish you enough pain so that smallest joys in life appear much bigger.
    I wish you enough gain to satisfy your wanting.
    I wish you enough loss to appreciate all that you posses.
    I wish you enough hellos to get you through the final Goodbye.
    My friend , I wish you enough.

    • 我愿你有足够的阳光，让你保持乐观；
      我愿你有足够的雨水，让你更加感激阳光；

    • 我愿你有足够的快乐，让你精神激昂；
      我愿你有足够的痛苦，让你感到生命中微小的欢乐也能带来幸福；

    • 我愿你收获够多，满足你的需要；
      我愿你失去够多，让你珍惜眼前的拥有；

    • 我愿你得到足够的问候，伴你挺过最后的永别。
      我的朋友，我愿你应有尽有，尽享人生。
